Ultimate Frisbee BYU CHI Ultimate

BYU Ultimate is a collegiate ultimate team from Provo, UT. Student Athletes from BYU compete in USA Ultimate's collegiate season and championship series each school year. Although not officially recognized by the university, USA Ultimate recognizes our team as the official team to represent BYU each year during the college championship series.

Club Championships The Orem Killjoys

The Utah Killjoys are a club ultimate team based in Orem, UT. The team competes in the Club Men's Division of USA Ultimate (USAU). Utah Killjoys moved up to #13 (from #16) in our Club Men's Power Rankings on November 3rd, 2021.
